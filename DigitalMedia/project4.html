Introduction

In this Section you will test your Creative Writing Abilities by wqriting a short story about the random prompt given to you after you click the button. Whatever prompt you get, write about it. It may just be a word or an idea. It's up to you to interpet and creatively come up with a story. My advice: Do not overthink it, just write what comes to mind. After you write your story, Read it out loud. How does it sound to you? Then you can figure out how you want to expand your story. What works? What doesn't work?
Be Creative!
